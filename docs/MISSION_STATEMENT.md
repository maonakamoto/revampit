# Revamp-IT Mission Statement

## Official Mission

**Revamp-IT is a Swiss nonprofit organization dedicated to enabling free exchange of technology between individuals and groups, promoting open-source hardware and software as the ideal form of human collaboration.**

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Free Exchange of Technology

We enable **free, nonprofit exchange** of technology between individuals and groups. Technology includes:

- **Hardware**: Old computers, computer parts, synthesizers, DJ controllers, peripherals, bicycles, furniture
- **Software**: Old proprietary software given away for free, open-source software
- **Resources**: Compute resources (servers), memory resources (storage), cloud infrastructure

**Key Principle**: All technology redistribution happens on a **nonprofit, free basis**.

### 2. Open Source Advocacy

**Goal**: Enable open-source hardware and software to compete fairly with proprietary solutions.

**Philosophy**: 
- Open source is the **ideal way for humans to collaborate**
- As development becomes more decentralized (can be done without big teams), open source will **ultimately exceed the capacities of big, centralized corporations**
- The most beautiful way for this to happen is through **technological collaboration and exchange beyond national boundaries**

**Vision**: Make technological collaboration and exchange easy and simple, transcending national boundaries.

### 3. Subscription Exchange & Transition Support

**When Subscriptions Are Needed**:
- Facilitate easy community funding of subscriptions
- Enable individuals to support each other's technology needs
- Provide transition path from proprietary to open-source

**Open Source First**:
- Maintain open-source alternatives until they reach sufficient maturity
- Provide computers equipped with **fully open-source AI and blockchain solutions**
- Rank technology upgrades by **environmental impact**

### 4. Environmental Impact Awareness

**CO2 Measurement**:
- Measure all electricity use: toasters, TVs, computers, AI, robotics, food production
- Enable individuals to understand their **true environmental impact**
- Rank technology upgrades by environmental impact

**Sustainability Focus**:
- Promote environmentally conscious technology choices
- Support circular economy through technology redistribution
- Reduce e-waste through refurbishment and reuse

### 5. Education & Digital Inclusion

- Technical education on Linux, open-source software, hardware repair
- Workshops on sustainable technology practices
- Digital inclusion programs for underserved communities
- Community building and knowledge sharing

## Nonprofit Status

Revamp-IT operates as a **Swiss nonprofit organization** with the following principles:

- ✅ **No Profit Distribution**: All surplus funds reinvested into mission
- ✅ **Public Benefit**: Activities serve public good
- ✅ **Non-Commercial Primary Purpose**: Technology redistribution, not profit generation
- ✅ **Transparency**: Financial records and activities are transparent
